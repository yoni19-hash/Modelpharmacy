About Us

Modelpharmacy was founded in 2010 with a mission to make quality healthcare accessible throughout Addis Ababa. Over the past decade, our passionate team has grown to serve people across 15 branches, while always putting patients first.

Our core values guide us every day:

Compassion & Respect
Professional Excellence
Community Commitment
Innovation in Healthcare

We’re proud to be a homegrown business supporting families, providing trusted advice, and bringing new technology—like e-prescriptions and SMS refills—to improve your pharmacy experience.
